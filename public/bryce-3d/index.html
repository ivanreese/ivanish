Bryce 3D

Bryce 3D was the first 3D graphics program I ever learned in depth. My parents bought me a copy as a birthday present when I turned 12 years old, and I took to it with an obsessive passion.

Below, you will see the very art made by 12-year-old Ivan. They all have watermarks, because I ripped these photos off my first ever website (which is still online). When I get back to Canada, I'll see if I can find "clean" versions.
