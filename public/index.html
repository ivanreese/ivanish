Personal History
In my teens I self-taught 3D animation, thinking that I'd rather enjoy a career as a computer artist. Thankfully, just before art college destroyed my life for the wrong muse, I realized my passion was for the technology, not the artistry. So I ripped my career in half: computer programming for the fortune, and music/art for the fame. Thus, the stuff featured on this site sits in the space bounded by these interests — software, music, art, and their mutant offspring. Granted, I have neither fame nor fortune, but if you only aim for Mars, you'll never get to M33.
