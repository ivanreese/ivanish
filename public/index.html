Personal History

In my teens I self-studied 3D animation, under the foolish belief that I'd enjoy a career as a computer artist. Fortunately, right as art school was about to ruin my life for the wrong muse, I realized my talent was for the technology, not the imagery.

I flipped my career inside-out, and became a professional art computerist. The work on this website was found along the path of that pursuit: concept games, algorithmic music, glitch art, and their mutant offspring.
