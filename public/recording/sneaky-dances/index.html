Sneaky Dances

This is an album I started in spring of 2014, and am still working on. A few of the songs are, indeed, sneaky fun. A few of them are sadly dumb. Le sigh.

When will the album be finished? Who knows. Can you hear some of it now? Caveat emptor. If you are going to listen — and I neither suggest that you do nor don't — note that Will-O-Wisp is my favourite song I've ever made.

Here's a video by my dear Trevor Kjorlien, in which he fulfills the annual Halloween box-costume tradition with aplomb. The song is titled "This Song Isn't About Anything" and — you guessed it — it's a work-in-progress cut from this work-in-progress album.
