History
In my teens I taught myself 3D animation, angling for a career as a ***computer artist***. But art school didn't mesh with me, and I realized my joy came as much from technology as artistry. So I flipped myself inside-out, fashioning a career as an ***art computerist***, if you will. I eventually fumbled my way into a community of outsider computer researchers, and I couldn't be happier.
