Photos

The album was burned on a blank white CD, wrapped in some paper that I coloured with incandescent paint and pencil crayon. This was then wrapped in folded onion paper, with a hole ripped through exposing the paint. The whole thing was held together with a round green toothpick skewered through the middle of the CD. I don't think I have any copies of the album left, which… worries me, a little. I like to keep at least one or two, for the memories.
