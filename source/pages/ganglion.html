type: Project time: 2005 through 2011 --- ! Ganglion

This one is big. Ganglion was my musical alias from 2005 through the end of the decade. It spanned across about a dozen albums and several live acts. Ultimately, I changed the name to Spiral Ganglion in the early 2010s, since there were more and more other projects using the name Ganglion. Now there's even a K-pop idol group!

Eventually, I'll post a detailed history here. Until then, you should consult the Wikipedia article.
