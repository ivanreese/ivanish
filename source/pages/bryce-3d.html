type: 3D time: Immemorial --- ! Bryce 3D

Bryce 3D was the first graphics program I learned in depth. My parents bought me a copy as a birthday present when I turned 13 years old, and I took to it with an obsessive passion. Every day after school, I'd come home and spend the entire rest of the evening playing around, making silly space opera-themed renderings.

Below, you will see the very art made by 13-year-old Ivan. They all have watermarks and compression artifacts, because I just stole these jpgs from my first website. Take that, child me.
